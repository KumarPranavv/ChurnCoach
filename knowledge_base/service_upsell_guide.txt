## UPSELL PRIORITY MATRIX (by customer data)
Given a customer's data, recommend in this order:
1. IF OnlineSecurity="No" AND DeviceProtection="No" AND TechSupport="No" → Protection Suite (highest retention impact)
2. IF InternetService="DSL" → Fiber upgrade (highest revenue impact)
3. IF StreamingTV="No" OR StreamingMovies="No" → Streaming bundle (high engagement)
4. IF Contract="Month-to-month" AND tenure > 6 months → Contract upgrade (highest LTV impact)
5. IF PhoneService="No" AND SeniorCitizen=1 → Phone addition
6. IF OnlineBackup="No" → Backup as easy add-on

## TIMING RULES
- Best: After successful support resolution or positive interaction
- Worst: During complaints, billing disputes, or outages
- Post-support: Wait 48 hours after issue resolution
- Onboarding window: 30-90 days after signup (engaged but not committed)
- Renewal window: 30 days before contract expiration
- NEVER upsell more than one service per interaction
