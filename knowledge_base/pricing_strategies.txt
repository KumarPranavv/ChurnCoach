## PRICING RULE 8: Hardship Relief Program
Eligibility: Any customer expressing financial difficulty
Discount by Data:
  - IF MonthlyCharges > $80: Up to 50% reduction for 3 months
    → Example: $90/month → $45/month for 3 months = saves $135
  - IF MonthlyCharges $50-$80: Up to 40% reduction for 3 months
  - IF MonthlyCharges < $50: Up to 30% reduction for 3 months
  - Gradual step-back to normal pricing over following 3 months
Conditions: One per 24-month period. Maintain minimum one service.
ROI: 72% of relief customers remain after period ends.

## PRICING RULE 9: Streaming Bundle Discount
Eligibility Data Match:
  - InternetService ≠ "No"
  - StreamingTV = "No" OR StreamingMovies = "No" (at least one missing)
Offer:
  - IF neither streaming: Both services at 25% off combined price
  - IF one streaming active: Add second at 50% off for 6 months
  - IF InternetService = "Fiber optic" AND no streaming: 30% streaming bundle discount
    → "Maximize your fiber speed with HD streaming"
Expected Impact: Streaming customers churn 20% less (content creates stickiness)

## PRICING RULE 10: New Customer Protection Rate
Eligibility Data Match:
  - tenure < 6 months
  - MonthlyCharges > $70
  - Contract = "Month-to-month"
Offer:
  - Rate lock at current MonthlyCharges for 18 months (no increases)
  - Free premium service trial (one of: OnlineSecurity, TechSupport, StreamingTV)
  - IF tenure < 3 months: Add $20 welcome credit
Conditions: Automatically applied when churn probability > 0.5
ROI: Acquisition cost of new customer is 5-7x retention cost. Protecting early = highest ROI.

## GENERAL PRICING GUIDELINES
- Total combined discounts: max 35% of base MonthlyCharges
- All discounts have clear end dates (except senior discount)
- Priority: retain high-MonthlyCharges customers first (highest ARPU)
- Review discount effectiveness quarterly
- Escalation: Agent → Supervisor → Manager → Director (for discounts > $35/month)
- Calculate exact savings for customer: always show dollar amounts, not just percentages
